Welcome to this course on rosacea.

This course provides an overview of rosacea as a common skin condition encountered in practice and explores how it presents, how it can be recognised, and how it can be managed safely. Throughout the module, learners will cover key characteristics of rosacea, identify red flags, review treatment pathways, and work through practical case studies to support understanding.

The focus is on building **knowledge, confidence, and safe decision-making** when working with clients who may present with rosacea.

## What this course will give you

By the end of this course, you will be able to:

- Understand rosacea beyond redness
- Assess, classify, and manage rosacea confidently
- Apply your knowledge in real clinical practice
- Work in a way that is aligned with NICE guidance

Each section builds on the last, so by the end of the course you are not just repeating protocols, you are making informed, evidence-based decisions about real clients in front of you.
