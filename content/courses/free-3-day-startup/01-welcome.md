Before you learn skills, build your client base, or shape your business, you need clarity. **Clarity on who you are, how you want to show up, and what direction truly aligns with you.**

Most new aesthetic practitioners skip this stage. They jump straight into trying to be "everything", every treatment, every trend, every client. And that's where overwhelm begins.

**This mini course is your pause.**
Your reset.
Your chance to understand the deeper layers of your identity as a practitioner, so every decision you make from here feels lighter, clearer, and more aligned.

## The three pillars

Over the next 3 days, you'll explore three foundational pillars of the 5K Formula:

**Day 1: Your Niche Awareness**
You'll begin noticing the subtle clues that reveal where you naturally belong in aesthetics, without pressure or forced decisions.

**Day 2: Your Brand Energy Awareness**
You'll explore the emotional experience your brand creates, and how clients begin trusting you long before they ever meet you.

**Day 3: Your Profit Awareness**
You'll discover the earliest signs of aligned, sustainable growth, and why **profit is a byproduct of clarity, not complexity**.

Each day includes a gentle exercise designed to help you gain immediate clarity, without overwhelm. And throughout the mini course, you'll start to see how your identity, your brand, and your business direction are all connected, forming the foundation of the career you're building.

## How to move through these 3 days

**This journey is intentionally soft, spacious, and supportive.**
No pressure. No expectations.
Just quiet insights that begin shaping a practitioner identity you can confidently grow into.

If you allow these three days to guide you, you'll walk away with something most early-stage practitioners never have:

- Awareness of your natural strengths
- Clarity on your emerging direction
- A deeper connection to the practitioner you're becoming

**You're not here to fit into the industry.**
**You're here to find your place within it.**

Let's begin unlocking that together.
