This is a free, 3-lesson taster of the full paid course. It's designed for practitioners who treat skin and want to **stop reaching for the same anti-redness regimen** on every rosacea presentation.

If you've ever felt the quiet frustration of:

- Clients whose redness fades and then comes back, again and again
- Plans that calm the surface but don't change the underlying condition
- Not being sure which subtype is in front of you
- Treatment-resistant rosacea that doesn't respond to the textbook plan

…then this short course is for you.

## What you'll get from these three lessons

In **20 minutes total**, you'll walk away with:

- A clear, science-led look at rosacea as a **barrier-and-microbiome condition**, not just a redness problem
- The **four NICE-recognised subtypes** and why each one needs a different pathway
- A clear next step if you want to learn the full clinical-reasoning approach

This isn't a sales pitch. The science in Lesson 2 is real, useful, and yours to keep — whether or not you ever take the paid course.

## Why I built this

Rosacea is one of the most under-treated skin conditions in private clinic practice in the UK. It's not because practitioners don't care — it's because **the model most of us were taught is incomplete**.

Most clinic plans treat rosacea as redness + flushing. They miss that rosacea is a **vascular, neurological, immunological, and barrier-driven condition** — and that anti-redness alone almost never holds.

The full **Rosacea Beyond Redness** course is **NICE-aligned, evidence-led, and built for clinical reasoning** rather than borrowed protocols. The Mini gives you a taste of the approach. If it lands, the door's open.
