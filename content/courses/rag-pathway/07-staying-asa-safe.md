## What this module covers

Visibility without compliance creates vulnerability. This module covers the ASA and CAP code rules as they apply to cosmetic interventions, the claims that are simply not permitted (including naming prescription-only medicines such as Botox), the rules around before-and-after imagery, influencer and gifted-content compliance, how to audit your social media language, and the rules of implication and inference that catch many otherwise careful practitioners out.

The goal is a marketing practice that grows your reputation rather than quietly putting it at risk every time you post.
