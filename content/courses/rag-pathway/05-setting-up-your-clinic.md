## What this module covers

This module focuses on the practical set-up that the regulators, insurers, and local authorities will look at if they ever need to. It covers premises licensing as it currently stands and where it is heading, local authority and council requirements, infection control standards, the documentation you need on file, how to structure your insurance correctly, the thresholds at which CQC registration becomes relevant, and how to work with a medical supervisor where applicable.

The aim is to give you a clear, sequenced checklist for setting up — or auditing — a clinic so that the foundations match the reputation you are building on top of them.
