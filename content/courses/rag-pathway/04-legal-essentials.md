## What this module covers

This module sets out the legal and regulatory baseline that applies to non-surgical aesthetic practice in England. It moves from the headline frameworks down to the practical questions that decide whether your practice is defensible: what your insurer is actually covering, where the boundary between medical and non-medical sits, and what the relevant bodies expect you to be able to evidence.

Topics covered include current England-only regulatory expectations affecting non-surgical aesthetics, professional standards, training requirements, insurance terms and exclusions, scope of practice, JCCP guidance, MHRA expectations on devices and prescription-only medicines, the implications of the Health and Care Act 2022, and the ASA / CAP advertising rules as they intersect with legal scope.
