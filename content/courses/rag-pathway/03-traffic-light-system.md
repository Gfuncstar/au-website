## What this module covers

The Traffic Light System is the navigational backbone of the entire RAG Pathway. It gives you a shared language for assessing where any decision — a treatment, a piece of marketing, a training certificate, a premises set-up — sits on the spectrum from clear risk through to demonstrable best practice.

By the end of this module you will be able to identify what puts your licence, insurance, or reputation at risk (Red), what requires improvement, evidence, or clarification (Amber), and what demonstrates the kind of professional maturity that protects and grows your reputation (Green).

The module is built around the four critical elements that the regulators look at — risk, competence, premises, and oversight — and the principle that runs through the whole programme: **if you cannot justify it on paper, you cannot defend it in practice.**
