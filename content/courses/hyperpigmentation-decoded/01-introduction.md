Welcome to **Hyperpigmentation Decoded**.

Hyperpigmentation is one of the most common reasons a client books in for skin work in the UK, and one of the most consistently mis-treated. The protocols are everywhere. The results don't hold.

Most plans fail in the same places. They treat surface-level pigment with topicals built for the wrong depth. They reach for in-clinic procedures that set off the very pigment they're trying to clear. They ignore the systemic driver underneath the breakout, then blame compliance when the client doesn't return.

This course was built for the practitioner who already knows the basics and wants the **layer above**. The face-reading framework. The depth-and-driver assessment. The post-procedure protocol that prevents rebound. The consultation script that holds the client through the slow middle of the protocol.

It is **NICE-aligned** where guidance applies, **clinically defensible**, and written from inside an aesthetics clinic that treats hyperpigmentation every week of the year.

## What this course will give you

By the time you finish:

- You can sort a new hyperpigmentation client into the right protocol track inside the first thirty seconds of the consultation
- You can read the **chromophore depth** off a face and explain it to the client in plain language
- You can ask the seven-question history that surfaces the real systemic driver in fifteen minutes
- You can sequence topicals for tolerance, not potency, on any Fitzpatrick
- You can choose the right in-clinic modality for the presentation in front of you, and refuse the wrong one with confidence
- You can hold the client through the twelve-week timeline that hyperpigmentation actually needs

This is **not** a hands-on technical training. It is the clinical reasoning underneath the technique. It pairs with whatever you already do in chair.

## How the course is structured

Eight modules, sequenced so each one builds on the last:

- **Module 1.** The five presentations of hyperpigmentation, on sight
- **Module 2.** The chromophore map: depth, type, distribution
- **Module 3.** The hormonal axis of melasma
- **Module 4.** UV, visible light, and the photodamage timeline
- **Module 5.** Active sequencing
- **Module 6.** In-clinic modalities
- **Module 7.** Preventing rebound
- **Module 8.** The consultation script and the twelve-week timeline

Plus a real-case studies module, a NICE-aligned summary, and a Certificate of Completion at the end.

## How to take it

You can finish the course in a weekend, or stretch it across a fortnight. Each lesson opens with an audio intro and runs as an editorial article. The next lesson queues up automatically. You will not be tested on memorisation. You will be tested on whether you can run a tighter consultation.

When you are ready, move on to the next lesson.
