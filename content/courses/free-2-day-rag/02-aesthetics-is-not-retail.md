One of the most dangerous misunderstandings in UK aesthetics is this:

> "I'm providing a service — like hair or beauty."

**You are not.**

The moment you:

- Alter skin
- Penetrate tissue
- Use devices with physiological impact
- Influence someone's body image or medical risk

You step into a **health-adjacent professional space**.

## Why this matters

Retail mistakes cost refunds.

Aesthetic mistakes can cost:

- Injury
- Insurance refusal
- Council enforcement
- Professional complaints
- Permanent reputation damage

This is why regulation exists — not to block you, but to protect the public.
