This section of the programme is designed to give aesthetic practitioners the **confidence, structure, and systems** needed to:

- Protect their diaries
- Reduce lost income
- Keep their calendars consistently full

In aesthetics, time is one of your most valuable assets — and how you manage **cancellations, no-shows, and pre-booking** has a direct impact on the stability and profitability of your business.

This course teaches you how to operate like a high-performing, fully booked clinic by putting strong frameworks in place.
