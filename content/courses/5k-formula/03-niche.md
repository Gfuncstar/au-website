## Understand your Clinical Core (Niche)

Your expertise forms the **first pillar of your Clinical Core**. This is the foundation of what you do exceptionally well and what sets you apart from other practitioners in the industry.

Your expertise is **not defined by how many treatments you offer**, but by the **quality and consistency of the results you deliver**. This pillar invites you to take an honest look at where your true strengths lie — clinically, technically, and creatively.

When you understand your expertise, you're able to build a brand rooted in **confidence, clarity, and authority**. Clients trust practitioners who know exactly what they excel in, and this clarity becomes the first step in positioning yourself as a specialist.

But how do you find your niche? The biggest stumbling block for many people is that they do not know how to find it. So let's delve in this deeper.

## The Niche framework

Your Clinical Core sits at the intersection of four questions:

- **Discover where you naturally belong** — the work you gravitate toward without being asked.
- **What you bring and the strengths that shape your direction** — your clinical, technical, and creative edge.
- **What makes you unique** — the perspective, story, or method only you can offer.
- **Your preferred client type** — the person you want to be fully booked with.

Where these four overlap, you find your **unlock**.

## The three pillars of the Clinical Core

**Key point:** Your niche is not a single treatment. It is the meeting point of three honest answers about your work.

**1. Pillar One — Expertise**

Your clinical strengths. The treatments you can deliver consistently, safely, and exceptionally well. Where your skill is measurably above the market average.

**2. Pillar Two — Excitement**

What energises you. The work you would still do if money were no object. Excitement is what protects you from burnout and keeps your craft sharp over years, not months.

**3. Pillar Three — Economics**

What pays back. The treatments and client groups that produce healthy margins, repeat bookings, and referrals. This is where your business becomes sustainable.

**Why this matters:** A niche built on only one pillar collapses. Expertise without economics is a hobby. Economics without excitement is burnout. Excitement without expertise is risk. The Clinical Core lives where all three overlap.
