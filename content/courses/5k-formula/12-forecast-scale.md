**Forecast and Scale** is a strategic growth module designed to help aesthetics business owners move from **reactive income to predictable, scalable profitability**.

This module teaches practitioners how to:

- Understand their numbers
- Anticipate future demand
- Plan for growth
- Implement systems that allow the business to expand without sacrificing quality, client experience, or wellbeing

Instead of relying on hope or inconsistent booking patterns, this module empowers students to take control of their financial future through **clarity, planning, and strategic decision-making**.

**Key point:** Forecasting creates direction. Scaling creates sustainable growth. Together, they form the foundation of a long-term, profitable aesthetics business.
