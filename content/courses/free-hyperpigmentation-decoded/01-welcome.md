Welcome to the **Hyperpigmentation Decoded** Mini.

Hyperpigmentation is one of the most commonly mis-treated presentations in UK aesthetic clinics. Practitioners reach for the same brightening serum every time. Clients lose faith in the protocol at week six. The pigment comes back darker after summer.

This Mini exists for one reason: to give you the **face-reading framework** that makes the next pigmentation consultation a different conversation.

In twenty minutes, across three lessons, you will:

- Recognise the five distinct hyperpigmentation presentations on sight
- Understand why surface treatment alone often misses the depth that matters
- Run the seven-question consultation that surfaces the systemic driver in fifteen minutes

Plus you'll walk away with the **Hyperpigmentation Consultation Card**, a one-page printable reference you can use in clinic from your next appointment forward.

## Who this is for

UK clinicians, aestheticians, skin therapists, and prescribing practitioners who already see hyperpigmentation clients regularly. This Mini assumes you know the basics of melanogenesis and topical chemistry. It gives you the layer above that.

If you're brand new to skin work, this Mini will still land, but the language is pitched for someone who has run consultations before.

## What this is not

It is not a replacement for the full **Hyperpigmentation Decoded** course. The full course covers eight modules of treatment and protocol, including modalities, rebound prevention, active sequencing, and the twelve-week timeline. The Mini covers the **first move**: reading the case in front of you. The rest of the framework is in the paid course.

It is also not hands-on technical training. There are no laser settings, no peel percentages, no procedural protocols inside this Mini. Those decisions sit in the full course or in your existing technique training.

## How to take it

Three lessons, roughly 5–7 minutes each. Read in order. The framework builds across all three. By the end of Lesson 3, you will have the printable consultation card and a clear next step.

When you are ready, move on to Lesson 2.
