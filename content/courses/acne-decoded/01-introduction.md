Welcome to the **Acne Decoded** course.

Acne is one of the most common skin conditions in the UK, yet it remains one of the most misunderstood.

Often dismissed as a teenage issue, acne is in fact a **complex, chronic inflammatory condition** that can affect individuals at any stage of life, with significant physical and psychological impact.

This course has been created to move beyond surface-level advice and generic protocols. You will gain a **clear, science-led understanding of acne**, learn how to assess its underlying causes, and develop **effective, ethical treatment strategies** based on evidence-informed practice.

Whether you are treating clients professionally or expanding your skin knowledge, this course will equip you with the confidence to approach acne **strategically, safely, and successfully**.

## What this course will give you

Throughout this course, you will gain:

- A clear scientific understanding of how acne develops
- The ability to differentiate between acne types and severities
- Insight into UK-specific acne trends and challenges
- Practical knowledge of both home-care and in-clinic treatments
- The confidence to build individualised treatment plans
- An understanding of the emotional impact of acne and how to communicate sensitively with clients
- NHS guidance used by the NHS

Each section builds on the last, ensuring that by the end of the course, you are not just repeating protocols, you are making informed, evidence-based decisions.
