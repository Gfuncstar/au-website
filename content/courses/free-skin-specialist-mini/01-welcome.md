Welcome to **The Skin Specialist™ Mini**.

This is a free, 4-lesson taster of the full paid programme. It's designed for practitioners, aestheticians, advanced facialists, beauty therapists, prescribing clinicians, clinical nurses, who want to **stop consulting like a beauty therapist and start consulting like a skin specialist**.

If you've ever felt the quiet frustration of:

- Plans that look great on paper and fall apart by week six
- Knowing acne and rosacea well, but improvising on pigmentation, barrier, or scarring
- Spending 60 minutes in a consultation and still missing the question that would have changed the plan
- Borrowing protocols from social media because the textbooks don't translate to clinic

…then this short course is for you.

## What you'll get from these four lessons

In **30 minutes total**, you'll walk away with:

- A clear read on why most skin plans fail (and where the failure usually starts, long before the treatment)
- The structured **Skin Specialist consultation framework**, the same one used inside Visage Aesthetics
- A clear next step if you want the full multi-condition clinical pathway

This isn't a sales pitch. The consultation framework in Lessons 2 and 3 is real, useful, and yours to keep, whether or not you ever take the paid programme.

## Why I built this

The single biggest gap I see in UK aesthetics isn't technique. It's the **consultation**.

Most practitioners I've taught are clinically capable. They're competent with their tools. They've done the manufacturer training, they've done the CPD. What they don't have is a **structured clinical conversation** that surfaces the real driver of the condition in front of them, and that's why their plans don't always hold.

The full **Skin Specialist™ Programme** is the comprehensive multi-condition pathway, acne, rosacea, pigmentation, barrier, photodamage, scarring, treatment planning, defensible documentation. NICE-aligned end to end.

The Mini gives you the foundation: the consultation framework that anchors every condition module in the full course.
