The way I think about it is this. There's a version of your practice where the audit gave you a gap list and the gap list sits on your laptop. There's another version where you decide to close the list. Four weeks of structured work. The consent templates current. The photography logged against the treatment record. The complaints procedure written down. The ASA-safe marketing rhythm in place. The clinic is defensible end to end, and the licensing scheme that's coming arrives as paperwork, not as crisis.

The RAG Pathway is the bridge between the two. £499, lifetime access, eight modules, four weeks of structured work, including the templates and audit tools that turn the audit's gap list into your working practice.
