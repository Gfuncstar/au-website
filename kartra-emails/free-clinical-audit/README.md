The audit is an 8-section self-audit (not a daily lesson sequence). The cadence reflects this: E1 hands over the audit, E2 nudges them through the heaviest sections, E3 reframes what the audit has surfaced, then the offer pacing matches the other sequences.

| Email | Day | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
