## Cadence

The 3-Day Startup is three days of daily lessons, so E2 nudges Day 2, E3 nudges Day 3, and the offer pacing extends one extra day at the end (14-day span vs the 13-day Acne / Rosacea sequences) so the higher-ticket offer has room to breathe.

|---|---|---|

## Voice progression

- **E1, E2, E3** — Voice 2 (warm mentor, course-content register). Reader still consuming the free taster.

- **E7** — Voice 1 at its most direct. Honest invitation.
