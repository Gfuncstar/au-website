# E2 — Brand awareness (Day 2)

**Subject:** `Why most aesthetics marketing sounds the same`

**Preheader:** `Day 2 is ready. The one-line statement that turns "another injector" into "the practitioner I trust."`

---

## Body

Hi {first_name},

If you read Day 1, you'll already have a feel for where this goes.

Most aesthetics marketing sounds the same because most clinics are
trying to be the same. Every treatment, every trend, every client
who walks past. The result is an industry of practitioners
competing on price and the photograph from yesterday's training.
The clients can't tell the difference between any two clinics, so
they pick on convenience and discount, and the practitioners blame
the market.

The market isn't the problem. The framing is.

A clinic that knows what it is doesn't have to compete on price.
The one-line brand statement is the small, ruthless exercise that
turns a generalist clinic into one specific person's destination
practice. It takes about thirty minutes and it changes the next
twelve months of marketing decisions.

Day 2 walks through how to find that one line, and how to recognise
when you've got the wrong one.
