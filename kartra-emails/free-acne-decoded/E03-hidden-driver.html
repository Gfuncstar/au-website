Hi {first_name},

A confession. For the first three years I treated acne, I worked the way most aesthetics training teaches you to work. I looked at the skin, picked the protocol, and got reasonable results.

Reasonable was not enough. Some clients responded; some didn't; and I couldn't predict which would be which.

The pattern broke when I stopped looking only at the skin and started taking a different kind of history. Hormonal cycle. Sleep. Stress. Gut. Recent antibiotic courses. Dietary shifts. Skincare overlaps. Most of the answers I needed were on the consultation form, not on the face.

Lesson 3 of the mini walks through the consultation questions that change the protocol before you've even chosen a product. It is the single thing that moves a practitioner from guessing to knowing.
