## Body

Hi {first_name},

If you read Lesson 1, you'll already have a feel for where this
goes.

Most acne advice fails for the same reason: it treats acne as a
single problem with a single fix. Wash more. Use this serum. Add
this peel. Try this LED. The protocol changes; the framing
doesn't.

Acne is not one condition. It's a pattern that sits on top of
several different drivers. Hormonal load. Barrier disruption.
Microbiome shift. Inflammatory cascade. Sometimes one. More often
two or three at the same time. The reason most plans stop working
after six weeks is that the plan was built for the visible symptom,
not the driver underneath.

Lesson 2 takes you through what that actually looks like in
practice, with the bits that matter for the practitioner across the
treatment table from the client.
